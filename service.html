Our Services

Grid-tie solar systems

Grid-tie solar systems are designed for those who already have electric utility service, but wish to significantly reduce their electric bill.

Off grid solar systems

Off-grid systems work independently of the grid but have batteries which can store the solar power generated by the system.

Hybrid solar systems

This ability to store energy enables most hybrid systems to also operate as a backup power supply during a blackout, similar to a UPS.

Annual Maintenance Contract

Annual Maintenance Contract services considering the Emerson/Tata Liebert, PCI, APC, Hirel, Powerware forms on purchaser demand.

preventive maintenance Contract

Solar Service Centre recommends the engagement of a Service Contract for general maintenance and cleaning of any solar system it installs.

Smart security system

Security hardware includes cctv cameras, Video door phones, Smoke detectors, lighting, Sensors systems.
