About Our Plant

Arkain Power Technologies has always been provide sustainable, innovative and cost effective solution. Arkain Power Technologies is found by young entrepreneur and inspired from startup India and digital India.


Arkain Power Technologies currently offer following products and solutions to its clients

  1. Solar PV panels- Mono crystalline and poly crystalline
  2. Grid tied,off grid And hybrid inverters ranging from 1kw to 100kw
  3. MPPT and PWM solar charge controllers ranging from 12v to 415v and 5A to 120A
  4. Solar water heaters ranging from 100 LPD to 5000 LPD
  5. Uninterruptible Power supply(UPS) 1phase and 3phase
  6. Energy storage batteries ranging from 2.4ah to 300ah ( VRLA,T ubular ,Li ion)
  7. Smart CCTV security (CCTV , smoke detectors, access control,video door phone etc.)
  8. Advance Air conditioners, Solar power conditioners
  9. LED lights, street lights and solar street lights
  10. Solar mini home lighting systems
  11. Wind Power Systems
